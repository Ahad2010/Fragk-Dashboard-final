Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. What We Collect

When you use FRAGK, we collect: your name and email (from sign-up or Google login), profile photo (if you upload one or sign in with Google), match screenshots you upload, and the stats extracted from them (map, placement, kills, player names).

2. How We Use It

Your data is used to run your dashboard, calculate stats and leaderboards, show your team's shared roster to teammates, and let our support team help you when you send a query. We don't sell your data to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

Uploaded screenshots are sent to Groq's AI API to extract match stats. If you sign in with Google, authentication is handled by Clerk. These providers process data according to their own privacy policies.

4. Data Shared With Your Team

If you join a team using a join code, your name and match stats become visible to that team's other members and owner. Leaderboards show your name and stats publicly to all FRAGK users.

5. Data Storage

Your account info, match data, and uploaded screenshots are stored on our servers for as long as your account is active. Session tokens are stored in your browser's local storage to keep you signed in.

6. Your Rights

You can update your profile information anytime from Settings. To request account deletion or a copy of your data, open a Support ticket after signing in.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Continued use of FRAGK after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.

8. Contact

Questions about your data? Reach out through the in-app Support ticket system after signing in.
